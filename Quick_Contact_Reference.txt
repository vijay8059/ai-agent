═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
WHAT TO ASK WHEN YOU CONTACT THEM
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

PRICING:
□ Monthly rental cost breakdown
□ Total cost for [your duration]
□ Security deposit amount
□ Any hidden charges?
□ Current promotional offers/discounts

DELIVERY & SETUP:
□ Delivery timeline to your location
□ Installation and assembly charges
□ Do they deliver to your specific Bangalore locality?
□ Setup time required
□ Do they remove packaging?

FURNITURE QUALITY:
□ What styles available (modern, traditional, contemporary)?
□ Warranty or guarantee on items?
□ Condition of rented items (new, refurbished, used)?
□ Replacement policy if items are damaged

MAINTENANCE & SUPPORT:
□ What's included in monthly rental?
□ Free maintenance and repairs?
□ Response time for damage/repair requests?
□ Who handles repairs - them or third party?
□ Are replacement items provided during repairs?

FLEXIBILITY:
□ Minimum rental period?
□ Can you extend or reduce duration?
□ Early termination penalties?
□ Can you upgrade/downgrade items mid-contract?
□ Can items be replaced with other styles?

CANCELLATION & RETURN:
□ Cancellation charges if you want to stop early?
□ How is pickup arranged?
□ Who bears return costs?
□ What happens if items are damaged during return?

INSURANCE & DAMAGE:
□ What's the damage coverage?
□ What damage is considered "normal wear and tear"?
□ How much are damage charges?
□ Is damage waiver available?
□ What happens to stains, scratches, dents?

BEST PRACTICES FOR CONTACTING COMPANIES
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

TIMING:
✓ Call during business hours: 10 AM - 5 PM (Monday - Friday)
✓ WhatsApp evenings/weekends for faster response
✓ Call multiple times if first attempt is unsuccessful

PREPARATION:
✓ Have pen and paper ready to note details
✓ Prepare exact list of furniture needed
✓ Know your delivery address (specific Bangalore locality)
✓ Know your rental duration
✓ Write down specific questions before calling

DURING CALL:
✓ Be polite and patient
✓ Ask for specific quotation in email after call
✓ Request product catalog and images
✓ Ask for references/Google review links
✓ Note down the representative's name
✓ Ask about best time to visit showroom

AFTER CALL:
✓ Request quotation via email
✓ Compare all quotations side-by-side
✓ Check company reviews on Google Maps
✓ Verify with previous customers if possible
✓ Schedule showroom visits for top 2-3 companies
