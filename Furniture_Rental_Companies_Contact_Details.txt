================================================================================================
RED FLAGS TO WATCH OUT FOR
================================================================================================

⚠️  Hidden charges not disclosed upfront
⚠️  Unrealistic damage charges
⚠️  Difficulty in cancellation or early returns
⚠️  Poor customer reviews or ratings
⚠️  No clear insurance or damage coverage
⚠️  Delivery charges not included
⚠️  Inconsistent furniture quality
⚠️  Lack of transparent contract terms
⚠️  No maintenance or replacement clause
