## 🔍 SECTION 9: KEY FINDINGS SUMMARY

1. **MRP is identical** across Amazon and Flipkart for new iPhones — the difference lies in bank cashback, exchange offers, and loyalty perks.

2. **Flipkart edges out Amazon** by ₹1,000–₹3,000 on effective discounts due to more aggressive bank partnerships and better exchange valuations.

3. **eBay offers the best prices for refurbished iPhones** — up to 45% cheaper than Indian MRP, but import costs for Indian buyers must be factored in, reducing the net savings to ~30%.

4. **For Indian buyers**, Amazon and Flipkart are the clear winners for new iPhones due to warranty, trust, and return policies.

5. **For global/international buyers**, eBay remains unmatched for pricing, availability, and the breadth of iPhone models.

6. **eBay's Certified Refurbished program** (2-year warranty) is a superior alternative to Apple's ARS (AppleCare Refurbished) in terms of value.

7. **During sale events**, Flipkart can offer an effective price on iPhone 16 128GB as low as ~₹62,900–₹64,900 (after bank cashback + exchange + SuperCoins) — the best deal available in the Indian market.
